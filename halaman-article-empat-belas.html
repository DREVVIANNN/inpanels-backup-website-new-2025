Upcoming Collaboration With MOMOI LOVERS Community!

We are thrilled to officially announce an exciting new partnership that has been in the works — a special collaboration with the beloved Momoi Lovers Community, set to launch in June 2025!


This upcoming collaboration is more than just a celebration — it’s a tribute to the passionate energy, creativity, and love that the Momoi Lovers Community brings to the world of anime fandom. Known for their unwavering devotion to Momoi from Blue Archive, this community has captured hearts through vibrant fan creations, deep conversations, and a unique atmosphere of joy that makes every fan feel like part of a greater family.


Together, we aim to create a collaborative experience that reflects everything Momoi represents: fun, color, friendship, and love for the game. From themed content, exclusive visuals, interactive experiences, and limited-time features — this collaboration is designed to honor both the character of Momoi and the incredible fans who support her every day.


We invite you to get ready for:
  • Special Momoi-themed visuals and digital content
  • Fan art features and creator shoutouts
  • Events and activities co-hosted with the Momoi Lovers Community
  • A shared celebration space across platforms


    • Whether you're a long-time Momoi supporter or someone new to the charm of Blue Archive, this collaboration promises to bring out the heart of what fandom is all about — connection through shared love.


    Stay tuned as we approach the June 2025 release date, and follow us on all platforms to catch exclusive previews, community showcases, and more. This is more than a collaboration — it’s a celebration of what we can build together with fans who truly care.


    Let’s make June shine pink with Momoi energy!
