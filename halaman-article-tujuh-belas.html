New Update V4.0.5 InPanels.Dev Website!

We are excited to officially roll out Version 4.0.5 of the InPanels.Dev website — a comprehensive update packed with improvements, new content, and fresh collaborations that elevate the entire user experience across our platform.


What’s New in V4.0.5?
Bug Fixes & Interface Improvements

  • Verified Badge Hover Bug Fixed: We heard your feedback! The previously glitchy hover animation on verified badges has now been smoothed out. Whether you're viewing on desktop or mobile, the verified mark now displays perfectly with no visual inconsistencies.
  • Navbar Simplification: We've removed some unnecessary list items from the navigation bar to create a cleaner, more focused user interface. Navigation is now faster, and more intuitive — helping you get where you need to be, quicker.


    • New Collaboration: Noctavy7 Community
    We’re proud to announce our latest partnership with the Noctavy7 Community — one of the largest creative networks focusing on GFX manipulation and anime-style visual edits. Their work blends real-life inspiration with stylized visuals, and now has a home on InPanels.Dev. Explore exclusive content, collaborative visuals, and future community events directly on our platform!


    More Articles, More Knowledge
    We’ve significantly expanded our article library. The updated article system brings new insights, creative showcases, developer notes, and community posts — all easily searchable and readable. Whether you're a casual reader or a hardcore developer, there's something new for everyone to discover.


    And Much More...
    V4.0.5 is more than just fixes and features — it’s a step forward in our journey toward making InPanels.Dev not just a website, but a vibrant hub for creators, developers, and fans of visual design. We’ve also laid the groundwork for future event integration, faster loading times, and refined responsiveness across all devices.


    A Message from the Team
    Every update we push is a reflection of community feedback and our constant pursuit of creativity and quality. We’d like to thank you all for your support and passion — whether you're a contributor, a collaborator, or a loyal user. This update sets the stage for even more powerful changes coming soon!
