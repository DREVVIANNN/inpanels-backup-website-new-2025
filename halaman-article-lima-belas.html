About - MOMOI LOVERS Upcoming Collaboration

Momoi Lovers Community
A Friendly Gaming Hub, Founded by KanasukeMusic


The Momoi Lovers Community is a vibrant, heartwarming space created for everyone who loves gaming, anime culture, and above all — Momoi, the beloved character from Blue Archive. Founded with passion and care by KanasukeMusic, this community was built to be more than just a fan space — it’s a public home for players seeking fun, friendship, and shared experiences.


At its core, the Momoi Lovers Community exists to bring people together. Whether you’re a long-time gamer or just getting started, this community welcomes all kinds of players with open arms. Here, friendship comes first — it’s a place where you can laugh together, game together, and celebrate your love for Momoi and everything she represents.


What Makes Us Special?
  • Open to Everyone – No matter your background, playstyle, or fandom experience, you're welcome here.
  • Find Friends to Game With – Our members are always looking for new players to team up with, whether casually or competitively.
  • Built on Love for Momoi – At the heart of it all is a shared appreciation for Momoi — her energy, her design, her charm — everything about her that makes the community shine.


    • This isn't just a Discord server or a social page — it's a place where friendships grow, where fans support each other, and where gaming feels just a little more magical thanks to the spirit of Momoi.


    So if you’re looking for new friends to play with, want to immerse yourself in fan culture, or simply want a safe space to geek out about your favorite pink-haired idol — Momoi Lovers Community is waiting for you.


    Let’s play together, laugh together, and make memories.
