Introducing V4.0.1 InPanels.Dev Website!

We’re beyond excited to unveil the next major step in our journey: Version 4.0.1 of InPanels.Dev! This release marks more than just a routine update — it’s a reflection of our ongoing commitment to innovation, community-driven collaboration, and refined user experience. We've been hard at work listening to your feedback, optimizing our features, and polishing the interface to make every visit to InPanels.Dev more enjoyable, engaging, and seamless. From eye-catching design improvements to vital performance fixes, V4.0.1 delivers a smarter, cleaner, and more interactive platform that grows with you.


What's New in V4.0.1:
Added Screen Load Animation
A smoother, modern loading screen to welcome you when the site launches.


New Collaboration Page: Athena Family
We've introduced a dedicated page to showcase the Athena Family, our new collaboration hub built on real community values.


Fixed: Background Bug on User Pages
We’ve resolved an issue that made certain links unclickable due to conflicting user background elements. Navigation now works flawlessly.


Renovated: Bar Graph ➜ Pinned Post
We’ve transformed the homepage bar graph into a cleaner, more user-friendly pinned post section for better visibility.


Enhanced Title Styling
Visual upgrade to titles across the site, making them bolder, clearer, and easier to read.


Expanded Info: Wuthering Waves V2.4 Update
Dive deeper into the Wuthering Waves 2.4 update with detailed insights and event summaries added to our update logs.


Wider Echo Wuthering Waves Layout
Echo images are now displayed in a more spacious, visually pleasing format to enhance clarity and aesthetic appeal.


...and much more coming soon!


Thank you for continuing this journey with us.
We’re building InPanels.Dev not just as a website — but as a platform that evolves with the community.
Let us know what you think, and stay tuned for the next version!
