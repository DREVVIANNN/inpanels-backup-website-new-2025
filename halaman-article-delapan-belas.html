New Update V4.1 InPanels.Dev Website!

We’re proud to officially launch Version 4.1 of the InPanels.Dev website — a major step forward in our mission to support streamers, creators, and the communities they build.


This update isn’t just about visual polish — it’s about creating a more fluid, personalized, and enjoyable experience for everyone who visits the site.


Streamer Blogs: Fully Remade
We've completely overhauled the entire streamer blog system from the ground up. The new design features:

  • A modern, minimal layout that puts your content first.
  • Cleaner text rendering for better readability on all devices.
  • Better responsiveness across Android, tablets, and desktop.
    • Your favorite creators now have blogs that actually feel like their space — unique, expressive, and easier to browse.


    Bug Fixes & Optimization
    We heard you loud and clear! This update focuses on real problems that affected your experience:

  • Fixed the persistent Google Translate pop-up that interrupted the flow of mobile browsing. No more frustration.
  • Improved JavaScript logic behind UI elements like mailboxes, popups, and love buttons (likes).
  • Reduced horizontal scrolling issues on Android by reworking image overflow handling.
    • \ Everything now feels smoother, lighter, and more responsive.


    Background Text Effect — Aesthetic Upgrade
    One of the more subtle but impactful additions in V4.1 is our new background text system. Now, while scrolling through About sections, articles, or profiles, you’ll see softly placed keywords, forming a dynamic visual texture across the screen. These:

  • Don't interfere with content
  • Make your web experience more immersive and modern
    • It's design — with intention.


    This isn’t just a website — it’s a living, growing platform built with creativity at its core.
