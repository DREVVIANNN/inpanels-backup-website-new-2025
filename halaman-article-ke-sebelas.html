Wuthering Waves Update V2.4 What's New?

The long-anticipated update for Wuthering Waves, titled Version 2.4: “Lightly We Toss the Crown”, introduces a wealth of content enhancements, narrative expansions, and gameplay features that significantly enrich the overall player experience. This update marks a substantial evolution in both story development and world-building, and introduces a brand-new region alongside highly awaited characters and new mechanical systems.

V2.4 Release Date Information:
Version 2.4 is set to release on June 12, 2025, bringing in familiar and new characters, a continuation to the main story, a brand new area to explore, and more in this exciting update!

Cartethyia

  • Rarity: 5★
  • Attribute: Aero
  • Weapon Type: Sword

    • Available in the first event banner June 12, 2025 As a central figure in both the narrative and gameplay mechanics of Version 2.4, Cartethyia arrives with a blend of grace and combat prowess. Her design emphasizes elegance in motion, and she’s a character who balances narrative weight with in-battle efficiency.


    Lupa

  • Rarity: 5★
  • Attribute: Fusion
  • Weapon Type: Broadblade

    • Featured in the second event banner Time TBA A fierce and imposing warrior originating from Septimont, Lupa embodies the raw power and indomitable will of a seasoned gladiator. Her arsenal includes heavy-hitting attacks and area-of-effect abilities, allowing her to dominate the battlefield through overwhelming strength. Her lore reveals strong ties to the Great Agon and personal motives linked to the crown of champions.

    V2.4 New Region : Septimont
    Septimont

    New Region: Septimont – The City of Valor
    A grand and awe-inspiring territory known as Septimont makes its debut. This freshly unlocked region offers players the chance to explore a bustling metropolis steeped in a proud tradition of martial honor and public tournaments. Septimont functions as the epicenter of the Great Agon, an elite combat competition where warriors align themselves with their Echoes to compete for supremacy and glory. This locale presents not only scenic architectural marvels and natural landscapes, but also a strong sense of cultural identity and historical intrigue. Players can expect a rich fusion of battle-themed customs, regional storylines, and new environmental mechanics that challenge their mastery and exploration instincts.

    NEW Main Quest V2.4
    Act V – “Shadow of Glory”
    In this chapter, players will unravel the mysterious disappearance of Cartethyia, whose existence can only be traced through a unique resonance accessible by the main protagonist, Rover. To gain access to the deeper secrets of Septimont and its ruling elite, Rover must engage in and triumph within the prestigious Great Agon. The storyline takes a profound turn, emphasizing emotional depth, lost connections, and hidden truths.


    Act VI – “Flames of Heart”
    Continuing directly from the climax of Act V, this act promises an emotional and action-packed journey, full of pivotal revelations, high-stakes encounters, and an intensification of the conflict surrounding Cartethyia’s fate. It solidifies Septimont’s role as a narrative centerpiece and teases broader implications for the world of Solaris-3.

    Nightmare: Kelpie will take the center stage for this update's Tactical Hologram, bringing in a new kind of tough challenge that players can take it!


    Gameplay Improvements and System Features
    Main Quest Focus Mode
    A streamlined gameplay setting that allows players to immerse themselves in story missions without distraction. While this mode is active, interactions with other characters and side quests are limited to allow better narrative focus.

    In-Game Guide Depot
    A newly added support feature designed to assist players—especially new ones—with detailed guides on character builds, resonance synergy, and recommended team compositions. This addition enhances accessibility and empowers players to strategize effectively.


    Additional Notes
    This update continues to push Wuthering Waves forward as a rich, evolving live-service action RPG. With each patch, the world becomes more intricate and emotionally compelling. Version 2.4 is not just about new content—it's about deepening the player's connection to Solaris-3 and laying the groundwork for what lies ahead.
