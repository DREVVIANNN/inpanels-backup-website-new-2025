The Website has been update to version 3.7B! our latest update, packed with improvements to enhance your experience on the site. Here’s what’s new in version 3.7B:


Leaderboard Redesigned
We’ve replaced the traditional leaderboard on the homepage with a brand-new bar graph visualization. This makes it easier and more intuitive to see user rankings and progress at a glance, giving you a clearer and more engaging way to track top performers.


Bug Fixes For Navigation
Some users reported issues with the hamburger menu not opening properly. We’ve identified and fixed this bug, so now accessing all menu options should be seamless across all devices and browsers.


Back Button Error Resolved
There was an error occurring when users tried to use the back button while viewing articles in the user section. This has been fixed, ensuring smooth navigation without any interruptions or unexpected behavior.


We’re committed to continuously improving the site based on your feedback and making your experience better every day. As always, thank you for being part of our community and helping us grow! Stay tuned for more updates coming soon!
